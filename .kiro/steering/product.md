# Product Overview

Red-Salud is a healthcare platform providing telemedicine and medical consultation services. The application enables patients to access medical care online and manage their health information digitally.

## Core Features

- Multi-role authentication system (patients, doctors, administrators)
- Patient profile management with medical history
- Document management for medical records
- User preferences and privacy settings
- Activity logging and session management
- Payment methods and transaction tracking
- OAuth integration (Google)

## User Roles

- **Paciente (Patient)**: Primary users accessing medical services
- **Doctor**: Healthcare providers offering consultations
- **Admin**: System administrators managing the platform

## Language

The application is in Spanish (es_ES locale).
